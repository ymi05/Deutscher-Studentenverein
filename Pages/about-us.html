Our Misson

To spread German Culture throughout our university

To encourage friendship

To compel the adjustment of difficulties arising among members

To protect our members against slander or other attack from outsiders

To share in social enjoyments;

To perform friendly services for one another

To encourage intellectualism amongst the student corpus

To adhere to the virtues of liberality

Our Code of Ethics:

  • To respect each individual memberâ€™s freedom of speech and expression
  • To evade practicing discriminatory acts against any member.
  • To uphold your duties assigned and tailored to you by the cabinet.
  • To be charitable and diligent, even in face of impossible difficulties.
  • To remain loyal to the society.
  • To attend by duty all assemblies.
  • To always help your fellow members.
  • Do good, sympathize with the weak, shun the malevolent, but do not hate.
  • Talk with respect to your superior, with prudence to peers, with sincerity to friends, and with tenderness to the poor.
  • Avoid disputes and shun insults; on such occasions utilize your reason.
  • Read and make the most of your reading. Observe and imitate. Reflect and work. See that everything redounds to the benefit of mankind rather than your own benefit.
  • Never judge the actions of your fellow brothers and sisters, for God is the sole judge of their actions.
  • Always listen to the voice of your conscience.
  • Respect and be a gentlemen to all female members.
  • Be always content for everything, with everything, and of everything.
